The Best Movies According to Anirudh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 idiots

Great movie about 3 friends during their college time and how they meet years after they graduate. Rewinds you back to your college days.

Interstellar

Really cool sci-fi movie about wormholes, blackholes and time dilation.

Vikram

Fictional action and thriller movie about a drug bust.
